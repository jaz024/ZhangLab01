Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • Plain flour
  • water
  • sugar
  • chopped apple
  • salt
  • butter
  • powdered cinnamon
  • starch
  • lime
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Soften the butter, then add plain flour and sugar
  2. Use hand to rub to made the mixture granular
  3. Add water and stir the mixture into dough, wrap the bowl and refrigerate for 10 mins
  4. Add butter in the pan before fire, then add chopped apple, some lime juice, and sugar
  5. Add the starch when liquid start forming, then shut the fire when the liquid is mostly gone
  6. Add salt and powdered cinnamon, stir
  7. Use half of the dough to spread it on the mould, put the parched apples in, and use the remaining dough to cover
  8. brush the pie with egg mixture, then put it in the oven. Bake it for 30 minutes with 190Â°C
